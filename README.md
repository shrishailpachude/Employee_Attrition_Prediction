📊 HR Employee Attrition Analysis & Prediction

SQL • Power BI • Business Analytics

📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major business challenge that increases hiring costs, reduces productivity, and impacts team stability.
This project analyzes HR employee data using SQL and Power BI to uncover the key drivers of attrition, build attrition risk insights, and present actionable retention strategies through an interactive dashboard.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze historical HR data to understand why employees leave

Identify high-risk segments based on tenure, salary, department, job role, overtime, and work-life balance

Use SQL to calculate attrition metrics and patterns

Build a Power BI dashboard for leadership decision-making

Provide data-driven recommendations to reduce employee churn

🗂️ Dataset

File: HR-Employee-Attrition.csv

The dataset contains employee-level information including:

Demographics (Age, Gender, Marital Status)

Job details (Department, Job Role, Job Level)

Compensation (Monthly Income, Salary Band)

Work conditions (Overtime, Work-Life Balance)

Performance ratings

Attrition status (Yes / No)

🔍 Key Insights

Early-tenure employees (0–1 year) have the highest attrition risk

Low salary bands experience significantly higher attrition

Overtime + poor work-life balance strongly correlate with exits

Sales and certain operational roles show above-average churn

High performance ratings do not guarantee retention, indicating burnout and compensation issues
